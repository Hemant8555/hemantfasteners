Type of Fasteners|Complete Guide

by Hemant

Posted on January 1, 2019 at 12:00 PM

Fastener are universal components used to create non-permanent joints between two or more objects, these joints can be dismantled without damaging the objects/components.

There are generally two type of joints:
Permanent Joints: Joints that cannot be removed/dismantled like welded joints. If removed                                         forcely, there are heavy chances of damaging components.
Temporary Joints: Joints that can be removed as per our choice like screws in electronic                                        devices. Eg. We can disassemble mobile phone using screwdriver.                                                              Fasteners falls into this category.

Objects can be joined using different objects like rope,wire or cable but they do not count into fasteners because they have more additional uses, but the fasteners have only use of connecting objects rigidly and mechanically.

Fasteners comes in different shapes,sizes,quality and material. So you should understand about different types of fasteners and their uses, so you can chhode appropriate fastener for your project.

Types of fasteners:

The most common types are the screw, nut and bolt, possibly involving washers. Other more specialized types of threaded fasteners include captive threaded fasteners, stud, threaded inserts, and threaded rods.
